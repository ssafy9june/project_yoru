Moonlight

First Album by Yorushika

Overview

Moonlight (Japanese: だから僕は音楽を辞めた, romanized: Dakara Boku wa Ongaku o Yameta), often translated as "That's Why I Gave Up on Music" in English, is the first studio album by Yorushika.

It was released on the 10th of April 2019, featuring a total of 14 tracks.
